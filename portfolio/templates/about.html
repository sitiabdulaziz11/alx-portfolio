About Us

Finder is a platform that intends to connect lost individuals with their loved ones. There are many scenarios where individuals get lost or separated from their loved ones. like after school, after graduation or because of searching for job opportunities and so on. We understand the emotional and practical challenges faced by both lost individuals and we intend to provide a safe and efficient way for individuals to connect with their loved ones, no matter where they are. Join us in our mission to bring families and friends back together. Visit Finder and start your search today.
